Grand Ma's Recipes

1. Kukul Mas Curry (Chicken Curry)

A flavorful chicken curry with coconut milk and aromatic spices.

2. Fish Ambul Thiyal

A sour and spicy fish curry made with tamarind, spices, and a mix of Sri Lankan herbs.

3. Lamprais

A Dutch-influenced dish that includes rice, meat curry, sambol, and a boiled egg, all wrapped in a banana leaf and baked.

4. Sri Lankan Beef Curry

A rich, slow-cooked curry with beef, spices, and coconut milk.

5. Dhal Curry

A comforting and flavorful lentil curry made with yellow lentils, cooked with spices and coconut milk, commonly served with rice.

6. Kottu Roti

a popular Sri Lankan street food made by stir-frying chopped roti with vegetables, meat, and spices.
