Hoppers

Hoppers, or appa, are a popular Sri Lankan dish made from a fermented rice flour batter. These bowl-shaped pancakes have crispy edges and a soft, fluffy center. They are often served with accompaniments like dhal curry, sambol, or a fried egg placed in the center. A variation, milk hoppers, includes a touch of coconut milk for a creamy flavor. Enjoyed at any time of day, hoppers are a delicious and versatile staple in Sri Lankan cuisine.
